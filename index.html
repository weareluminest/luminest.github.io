For Parents First

We know the early years with an autistic child can feel overwhelming and beautiful at once. LumiNest offers therapeutic, research-backed digital tools designed specifically for parents raising children between the ages of 2 and 6. Our mission is to empower, guide, and comfort — every step of the way.

Why LumiNest?

  • Designed with love and science for early autism support
  • Gentle, interactive mini-games for children
  • Calm design, tested for sensory comfort
  • First women-led initiative of its kind in the UK

Our Vision

We’re more than a digital tool. LumiNest is a wellbeing studio, created with therapists, parents, and educators. We work to ensure that every child — and every parent — feels safe, seen, and supported.
